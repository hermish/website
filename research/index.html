Research

I'm currently doing research in Professor Tom Griffiths' Computational Cognitive Science lab and Professor Tania Lombrozo's Concepts and Cognition lab, at the intersection of computer science, psychology, and cognitive science. Our current work involves analyzing real-world big data to draw insights about what drives curiosity.
