Research

I'm currently doing research in Professor Tom Griffiths' Computational Cognitive Science lab (now at Princeton University) at the intersection of computer science and cognitive science. Last summer, I had the pleasure of working on a project to improve approximation guarantees for the semi-random maximum independent set problem.
